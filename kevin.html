Learning to Walk

By Carlos Serrano

In the night of August 6, 2015, Kevin Lu was going home on his motorcycle. It was his birthday. About 11:30 p.m on the Whitestone Expressway in Queens, NYC, an 18 wheeler truck struck him and ran over him. He does not remember anything about that but when he woke up in the hospital, doctors already had amputee his legs. Now Kevin is 32 years old, is using prosthetics since 9 months and is working hard, mentally and physically, to learn to walk once again.
